Where are all the tuition dollars going? UC Employee Salary Visualization

Objective

In order to create understanding where there is a lot of confusion, I will visualize how much the UC System is paying it's employees. Some Questions I hope to answer are:

  • What position makes the most money?
  • The least?
  • What percentage of total payroll goes to each Employee Category?
  • Which UC Pays the best?
  • Does being a lecturer mean you make a living wage?
